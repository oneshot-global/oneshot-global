3 Reasons to Choose OneShotCal

📅

No new place for your plans

OneShotCal writes events read from photos and screenshots directly into the Google Calendar you already use. There is no dedicated app to open and no sync setup with another calendar.

🌍

Accurate because it never "calculates" time zones

Overseas hotel emails mix local time, server time, and inconsistent formats — a classic source of time-zone conversion mistakes. OneShotCal does not convert times to UTC by itself. It identifies the location (city, country, airport code, or address) in the text and delegates the conversion to the world-standard time zone database inside Google Calendar. Daylight saving transitions — and exceptions such as Arizona and Hawaii, which do not observe daylight saving time — are covered by design, leaving no room for calculation errors.

🌐

Overseas plans, as they are

Japanese, English, German, French, and Spanish are supported. Confirmation emails from your travels abroad are translated and registered accurately in your calendar.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How is OneShotCal different from TimeTree?

A. TimeTree is a shared calendar app that stores events inside its own app, and using it alongside Google Calendar requires a separate sync setup. OneShotCal writes events directly into the Google Calendar you already use from the start. There is no new app to learn.

Q. Can it register overseas hotel confirmation emails?

A. Yes. OneShotCal identifies the location — city name, airport code, or address — in the text, determines the time zone automatically, and converts it using Google Calendar's standard time zone database. Events are registered accurately, including daylight saving time changes.

Q. Which languages are supported?

A. Japanese, English, German, French, and Spanish — five languages in total.

Q. Can it read kindergarten or school annual schedules (table format)?

A. We are working on it. Accuracy for complex grid-style layouts such as annual schedules is still being improved. Currently, OneShotCal registers one event per image.

Q. How much does it cost?

A. The free plan allows up to 3 uses per month. The premium plan costs $1 per month for up to 30 uses, and failed analyses do not count against your quota.

Q. What happens to uploaded images?

A. Images are deleted from our servers immediately after analysis. Google user data obtained by the service is never used to train AI/ML models.
